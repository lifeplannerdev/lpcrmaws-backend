## Requirements
### Requirement: Staff Page Visual Excellence and Usability
The Staff Page SHALL provide a complete, optimized, and highly usable interface that meets production-ready standards, featuring a premium dynamic design.

#### Scenario: User navigates to Staff Page
- **WHEN** the user navigates to the Staff Page
- **THEN** the system SHALL display the complete list of staff members with modern styling, clear typography, and responsive layouts.

### Requirement: Staff Details Comprehensive View
The Staff Details view SHALL display all staff-related data in an organized, easily digestible format.

#### Scenario: User views a specific staff member
- **WHEN** the user clicks on a staff member in the list
- **THEN** the system SHALL open the Staff Details page, showing their profile, contact logic appropriately applied, and available tabs (e.g., Profile, Assets).
